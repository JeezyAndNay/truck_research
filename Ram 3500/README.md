# Ram 3500 — Cummins Diesel
*6.7L Cummins I6 · SRW · Crew Cab · 4x4*

The Ram 3500 with the 6.7L Cummins is the torque king of the 1-ton class. The inline-6 Cummins is one of the most respected diesel engines in the world with a commercial truck lineage going back decades. The Standard Output (CP3 pump) is the most worry-free used diesel buy in this segment.

## Quick Stats (2024, SRW Crew Cab 4x4 Diesel)
- **Max tow:** 37,090 lbs (conventional)
- **Torque:** 1,075 lb-ft (HO) / 850 lb-ft (SO)
- **Transmission:** Aisin AS69RC (HO) or 68RFE (SO)
- **Injection pump:** CP3 (all 2007.5–2018) / CP4.2 (SO + HO 2019–2020) / CP3 (all 2021+)
