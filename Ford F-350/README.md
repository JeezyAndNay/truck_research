# Ford F-350 Super Duty — Power Stroke Diesel
*6.7L Power Stroke V8 · SRW · Crew Cab · 4x4*

The F-350 Super Duty holds the highest tow rating in the 1-ton class at 40,000 lbs. The 6.7L Power Stroke V8 diesel (introduced 2011) is Ford's in-house diesel — designed and built by Ford, not outsourced. Post-2015 reliability is strong after early EGR and fuel system issues were addressed.

## Quick Stats (2024, SRW Crew Cab 4x4 Diesel)
- **Max tow:** 40,000 lbs (conventional hitch)
- **Power/Torque:** 475 hp / 1,050 lb-ft
- **Transmission:** TorqShift 10R140 10-speed
- **Injection pump:** CP4.2 (all years)
- **Off-road package:** Tremor (F-350 available)
