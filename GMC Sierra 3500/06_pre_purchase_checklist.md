# GMC Sierra 3500 HD AT4 — Pre-Purchase Checklist
*6.6L Duramax L5P · Allison 10L1000 · AT4 · SRW · Crew Cab · 4x4*
*Print or use on your phone at the seller's location*

---

> **Buy target: 2021–2023 Sierra 3500 AT4.** AT4 package only exists on 4th gen (2020+). First-year 2020 Allison 10-speed had shift calibration issues — TSBs addressed for 2021+. This checklist assumes 2021–2023 AT4. If looking at a 2020, verify Allison TCM software is current at a GM dealer before buying.

---

## Before You Go — Documentation

- [ ] Confirm model year is **2020 or newer** (AT4 only available 4th gen and up)
- [ ] Confirm **AT4 trim** — not all Sierra 3500s are AT4. AT4 has DSSV dampers, skid plates, DuraTrac tires, and specific badging
- [ ] Run VIN on [nhtsa.gov/recalls](https://www.nhtsa.gov/vehicle/recalls) — check for open recalls
- [ ] Run VIN on [vpic.nhtsa.dot.gov/decoder](https://vpic.nhtsa.dot.gov/decoder/) — confirm AT4, L5P Duramax, Allison 10L1000
- [ ] Pull Carfax or AutoCheck — accidents, flood, title issues, odometer rollback
- [ ] Request maintenance records — specifically Allison TranSynd 668 fluid history and oil change intervals
- [ ] Ask for original window sticker or build sheet (confirms AT4 package vs. a trim-level lookalike)

---

## AT4 Package Verification — Confirm Before Anything Else

- [ ] **DSSV dampers:** Multimatic-branded shocks at all four corners — not standard shocks. Verify by looking at shock body (Multimatic logo) or asking for window sticker
- [ ] **Skid plates:** factory steel skid plates should be visible under front of truck
- [ ] **DuraTrac tires:** Goodyear Wrangler DuraTrac LT285/75R18 — check sidewall markings. If different tires, ask what replaced them and why
- [ ] **AT4 badging:** interior and exterior AT4 trim badges. Not cosmetic-only — confirms the package
- [ ] **G80 rear locker:** standard on AT4. Test later in drive section

---

## Allison Transmission — Most Important Maintenance Item

- [ ] Confirm Allison 10L1000 (standard on all L5P Sierra 3500s)
- [ ] Ask for TranSynd 668 fluid change records — **only acceptable fluid; no substitutes**
- [ ] Under tow load interval: every 25,000 miles. Normal: 45,000–50,000 miles
- [ ] If no service records at high mileage: price in a fluid + filter service ($250–$400)
- [ ] Check fluid if accessible: should be red/pink and clean
- [ ] **2020 trucks:** ask if Allison TCM software has been updated at a dealer — early 10-speed Allison had shift calibration issues

---

## Injection Pump Confirmation

- [ ] Confirm 2020+ L5P = CP3 pump — no CP4 risk on any 4th gen Sierra with L5P
- [ ] If any year doubt: VIN position 10 — 'L' = 2020, 'M' = 2021, 'N' = 2022, 'P' = 2023, 'R' = 2024
- [ ] LML (2011–2016 Sierra) had CP4 — if you're somehow looking at an LML, stop and use the Ford checklist logic

---

## Exterior

- [ ] Panel gaps consistent — uneven gaps suggest prior accident repair
- [ ] All four wheel arches: rust check under paint (less common on newer trucks but check)
- [ ] Rocker panels: AT4 has specific trim — check for damage or road rash from off-road use
- [ ] Bed: check corners, floor, tailgate for rust or cargo damage
- [ ] Hitch receiver: no cracks, no severe rust inside tube
- [ ] 7-pin trailer plug: inspect pins for corrosion or damage
- [ ] **DuraTrac tires:** check tread depth (>4/32"), even wear across tread face, DOT date code under 6 years. These tires have ~50,000–65,000 mile life — note remaining life and price in replacement if near end
- [ ] All lights functional: headlights, brake, reverse, turn signals, marker lights
- [ ] Check AT4 skid plates from underneath: rock strike dents/cracks indicate off-road use — inspect more carefully

---

## Under Hood

- [ ] **Engine oil:** pull dipstick — amber/light brown (0W-20 dexos D). Milky/foamy = coolant contamination
- [ ] **Oil capacity is 8 quarts** — significantly less than Cummins; confirm dipstick reads correctly
- [ ] **Coolant:** DexCool (orange) at correct level. Milky = head gasket concern
- [ ] No visible coolant leaks around EGR cooler or hose connections
- [ ] **Water pump (2020 first-year trucks):** watch for weeping at pump snout — known early L5P issue carried into 4th gen early production
- [ ] Fuel filter housing: no leaks
- [ ] Battery terminals: clean and tight
- [ ] Turbo: no oil weeping; shaft play within normal range

---

## Under the Truck

- [ ] Frame rails: no cracks, weld repairs, or heavy rust
- [ ] **AT4 skid plates:** inspect all plates for cracks, severe dents, or torn mounting — indicates hard use
- [ ] Transfer case: no leaks
- [ ] Front driveshaft and U-joints: check for play/wear
- [ ] Front differential: no leaks
- [ ] Rear differential: no leaks. **G80 locker equipped** — confirm no leaks at locker actuator
- [ ] **DSSV dampers:** visually inspect all four for oil leaks (wet/oily shock body = leaking damper, ~$400–$600 each to replace)
- [ ] Exhaust: no rust-through, DPF intact
- [ ] DEF tank: no leaks, level accessible
- [ ] **Hitch height note:** AT4 sits ~2" higher than a standard Sierra — measure receiver height for correct ball mount drop before towing

---

## Interior & Electronics

- [ ] Connect OBD scanner — check for stored DTCs, particularly transmission or DEF codes
- [ ] No warning lights after engine warmup
- [ ] DEF gauge reads correctly, no DEF fault codes
- [ ] Air conditioning: blows cold
- [ ] Infotainment and backup camera: functional
- [ ] **MultiPro tailgate:** test all six positions functional — this is a Sierra-exclusive feature and the actuator can have issues
- [ ] 4WD selector: test 4H and 4L engagement
- [ ] **AT4 off-road mode:** if equipped with terrain modes, confirm they engage
- [ ] Seats, door seals: check for water intrusion staining

---

## Test Drive — Minimum 20 Minutes, Mixed Speeds

- [ ] **Cold start:** clean, no excessive white smoke after 2 minutes
- [ ] **Allison shifts:** smooth and confident through all gears. Hunting, harsh banging, or shuddering = fluid or calibration issue
- [ ] **Tow/Haul mode:** engage and confirm Allison holds lower gears under deceleration
- [ ] **Turbo:** smooth spool, no unusual whistling or surging
- [ ] **DSSV damper feel:** AT4 should ride noticeably better than a standard Sierra — if ride feels harsh or uncontrolled, suspect leaking dampers
- [ ] **No factory exhaust brake** — Allison Tow/Haul compensates; aftermarket available if needed
- [ ] **G80 locker test:** on a dirt surface, turn slowly in a tight circle — listen for the locker to engage (audible click/thud)
- [ ] **Highway cruise:** no vibrations, tracks straight
- [ ] **Braking:** firm pedal, no pulling, no pulsating
- [ ] **4WD:** 4H and 4L — clean engagement, no grinding
- [ ] **No new warning lights** after 15+ minutes of driving

---

## Truck-Specific Watch Items by Mileage

| Mileage | Items to Scrutinize |
|---|---|
| Any | Allison fluid type and service history |
| Any | AT4 package authenticity (skid plates, DSSV, DuraTrac) |
| 2020 trucks | Allison TCM software update confirmation at GM dealer |
| 50K+ | DSSV damper visual inspection for leaks |
| 100K+ | DSSV dampers likely need replacement soon (~$400–$600 each); EGR inspection; injector balance rates |
| 150K+ | Full pre-purchase diesel shop inspection recommended |

---

## Mileage Assessment

- [ ] **Under 80K** — confident buy range for 2021–2023 AT4 (realistic expectation for this age)
- [ ] **80K–150K** — acceptable with documented Allison fluid service and clean inspection
- [ ] **150K–200K** — acceptable with full records + DSSV damper assessment
- [ ] **200K+** — pre-purchase inspection mandatory; DSSV dampers will likely need replacement

---

## Pre-Purchase Inspection (any truck over 60K miles)

- [ ] Booked at a GM dealer or Duramax-specialist shop ($150–$200)
- [ ] Requested: compression test, scanner live data, Allison health check, DSSV damper inspection, physical lift inspection
- [ ] Allison fluid condition confirmed
- [ ] DSSV dampers checked for leaks — get replacement cost estimate if needed
- [ ] No active or pending fault codes

---

## Final Decision Gate

- [ ] AT4 package authenticity confirmed (skid plates, DSSV, DuraTrac) ✓
- [ ] Model year 2021+ confirmed (or 2020 with Allison TCM update confirmed) ✓
- [ ] CP3 pump confirmed (L5P, any 4th gen Sierra) ✓
- [ ] Allison TranSynd 668 service history confirmed or acceptable ✓
- [ ] Transmission shifts smooth, no hunting or shudder ✓
- [ ] DSSV dampers inspected — condition noted ✓
- [ ] DuraTrac tire remaining life assessed ✓
- [ ] No active DTCs ✓
- [ ] Mileage within acceptable range ✓
- [ ] Pre-purchase inspection passed (if applicable) ✓
- [ ] Price negotiated to reflect condition — DSSV replacement and tire life factored in ✓

> **All boxes checked? You're ready to buy.**
> **Any unchecked? Negotiate the item into the price or walk.**
